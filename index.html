The conversational AI redefining language learning

Meet Loree

Intelligent companion

Loree is an intelligent companion that lives inside your devices. She is fluent in all languages, and helps introduce language learning and culture into a child’s daily routine.

Native language exposure

Loree enters conversations with you and your child to learn about what made the day so special. Being a natural conversationalist, Loree is interactive and personable.

Customs and traditions through stories

Based on the conversations you have with her, Loree generates topical storybooks. Her books revisit the day’s experiences and introduce customs and traditions unique to your culture.

Technology

Loree isn't just a friendly face — She's a great listener who thinks on her feet.

Conversational UI

Talking to Loree is no different to talking to a real person. She responds, asks questions, and even laughs.

Machine Learning

Loree is smart. She pays close attentions to your child's needs as they continue to grow and learn.

AI Illustrations

Loree comes up with original stories and beautiful illustrations, with just a little help from your child.
